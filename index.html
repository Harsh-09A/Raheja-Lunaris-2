About Raheja Universal

Raheja Universal, led by Mr. Suresh L. Raheja and his sons Rahul and Ashish, has been a pioneer in real estate development since 1986. The company focuses on delivering high-quality, innovative projects using advanced technology and premium materials. Their leadership has set new industry standards by anticipating market trends and consistently exceeding customer expectations.

The company has evolved over time, with several name changes reflecting its growth. Initially incorporated in 1980, it became Raheja Universal (Pvt) Limited in 2012. Despite these changes, Raheja Universal has remained steadfast in its values, striving for excellence and setting milestones in the real estate industry.
